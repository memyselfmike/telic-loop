# Beep2B v4 — Premium B2B Lead Generation Website

A complete rebuild of beep2b.com as a modern, visually stunning marketing website inspired by the Nexus digital agency template (dark theme, bold typography, smooth animations, parallax effects). Powered by Astro for static performance, Payload CMS for content management, and Docker for zero-hassle setup.

## The Promise

A dark-themed, animation-rich B2B marketing website that makes beep2b.com look like a premium agency — not a template. Every section has smooth scroll-triggered animations, parallax backgrounds sourced from Pixabay, and the kind of visual polish that builds instant credibility. The BEEP methodology (Build, Engage, Educate, Promote) is presented as an interactive journey, not just a list of steps.

## What Success Looks Like

- `docker compose up` launches the entire stack (Astro dev server, Payload CMS, MongoDB)
- Dark theme with accent colors, inspired by the Nexus v3 template aesthetic
- Smooth scroll-triggered animations on every section (fade-in, slide-up, parallax)
- Hero section with bold headline, animated background, and dual CTAs
- All 7 pages ported with fresh copy and Nexus-inspired layout
- Payload CMS admin panel at /admin for managing blog posts, testimonials, and page content
- Blog with pagination, category filtering, and rich post pages
- Contact form with validation and success/error states
- Pixabay API integration for high-quality background images
- Responsive at 1280px, 768px, and 480px breakpoints
- Professional enough to deploy as the real beep2b.com
