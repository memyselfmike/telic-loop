# Bookshelf — Personal Book Tracker

A beautiful, fast personal book library where you can catalog your books, track reading progress, and instantly search across your entire collection.

## The Promise

You open Bookshelf and see your library — covers, ratings, reading status at a glance. You add a new book and within seconds it's searchable by title, author, genre, or even notes you've written about it. You can filter by "currently reading", "want to read", or "finished", and see stats about your reading habits.

## What Success Looks Like

- A visually polished web app that feels like a real product, not a prototype
- Add/edit/delete books with cover images (URL-based), ratings, notes, and reading status
- Instant full-text search powered by a dedicated search engine — not just SQL LIKE queries
- Reading status tracking: Want to Read, Currently Reading, Finished, Abandoned
- A dashboard showing reading stats: books per status, genres breakdown, average rating
- Everything persists across restarts — real database, real search index
- Runs via `docker compose up` with zero manual setup
